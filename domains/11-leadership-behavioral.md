## Working Across Time Zones

# Question

How do you manage working across different time zones?

# Senior-Level Answer

I manage time-zone differences through clear communication, documentation, and planned overlap hours.

I make sure tasks, blockers, decisions, and next steps are documented in shared tools instead of staying only in private chats. I also provide clear handover notes so other team members can continue work even when I am offline.

For important discussions, I use overlap hours. For normal updates, I prefer asynchronous communication.

# Example

```text
A good handover includes status, branch or PR link, pending items, blockers, and next steps.
```

# 15-Second Version

I manage time zones with documented updates, overlap hours for important discussions, async communication for normal updates, and clear handover notes.
